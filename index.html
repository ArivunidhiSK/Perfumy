About Us

Renee Cosmetics offers a diverse range of perfumes that combine premium quality with affordability, catering to various preferences and occasions. Their fragrances are known for their long-lasting scents, elegant packaging, and cruelty-free formulations.

    Key Fragrances:
    Bloom: A floral fragrance featuring notes of almond, tuberose, jasmine, sandalwood, vanilla, cocoa, and tonka. It's often compared to Carolina Herrera's “Good Girl” for its sweet and floral
    Dark Desire:A bold scent with woody and spicy notes, including musk, cedarwood, amber, vanilla, mandarin, grapefruit,pink pepper, cassis, red berries, and jasmine. Ideal for evening wear
    Oud Aspire: A luxurious blend with top notes of raspberry, orange, and caramel; heart notes of rose, agarwood, olibanum, patchouli, and violet; and base notes of amber, vanilla, and musk.
    Renee's perfumes are available in various sizes, including travel-friendly 15ml bottles and standard 50ml bottles, making them convenient for different needs. The brand has also collaborated with Bollywood actress Janhvi Kapoor as the face of their fragrance line, emphasizing their appeal to a younger audience.
